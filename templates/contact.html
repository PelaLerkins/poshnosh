Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking at least one week in advance for regular meal services, and 2-3 weeks for special occasions or events.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We can accommodate various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and specific allergies.

What areas of Vancouver do you serve?

We primarily serve downtown Vancouver, including Yaletown, Coal Harbour, West End, and Gastown. Contact us for service availability in other areas.

How does payment work?

For our flat rate + groceries packages, the service fee is charged weekly, and grocery costs are billed separately. All-inclusive packages are billed at a single weekly rate.
